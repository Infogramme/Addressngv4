Founding vision

Digital Federalism.
Sovereign infrastructure.

Address Nigeria rests on a coherent ideological foundation: Digital Federalism — the application of cooperative federalism principles to digital infrastructure design. Its intellectual lineage traces from Hegel through Awolowo to Barrister Bisi Adegbuyi.

The 774 Local Government Areas are the bedrock unit of governance. AiG-CTG is designed from the LGA node upward — each LGA a sovereign addressing authority within its domain.

01
Innovation is infrastructure
Nigeria’s reform agenda requires a universal, machine-readable address substrate.
02
Bottom-up, not top-down
Federated autonomy at the LGA level. National uniqueness at the top.
03
Private-sector traction first
CRYLID pilots generate proof and cashflow before public-sector adoption.
04
High-impact MVP over over-engineering
Built to minimum viable specification. Expanded only by proven demand.
